Welcome to Peculiar Consulting

PCL was founded in 1999. The Firm provides accounting, tax, and business advisory services to a variety of clients, including Individuals and Corporate entities, including non-profit organizations, and religious institutions.

The President of PCL is a qualified CPA. The Firm and/or its senior staff are members of various professional organizations, including:

  • The American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA)
  • The National Society of Accountancy (NSA)
  • Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA)
  • Greater Washington Society of Certified Public Accountants
  • Virginia Society of Certified Public Accountants

The Firm has received testimonials from our clients nationwide.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help clients maintain financial viability in the present while taking a proactive approach to achieve future goals. This requires open communication to reach an understanding of our clients' needs through research and sound analysis. PCL is dedicated to meeting these goals with high standards of excellence and professionalism.

We believe in dedication and commitment to our clients. Whether you are a current or prospective client, rest assured that clients who choose PCL receive competent and timely service.

Our Philosophy

  • Quality. At PCL, we are proud of our reputation for excellence. Our primary goal is to provide services of the highest quality to our clients.
  • Timeliness. A key to client satisfaction is the ability to meet their statutory and critical deadlines. We strive to complete our engagements in sufficient time, and within the agreed deadlines, to achieve maximum impact and meet the needs of decision makers and other users.
  • Responsiveness. PCL has effective mechanisms in place to anticipate clients' needs. We strive to respond with innovative, tailored solutions. Keeping communication channels open and listening to our clients is integral to our success.
  • Value. Finally, PCL emphasizes affordable services. Combined with our commitment to quality, timeliness, and responsiveness, our affordable prices make us a true value in every sense of the word.
